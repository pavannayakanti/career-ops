### Sell senior without lying

- **Open with scope:** "I've run platform engineering programmes at VP scope for three of the largest financial institutions in North America — ADP, Charles Schwab, RBC. The developer platform at each of them was the thing I built, instrumented with DORA, and handed over as a product."

- **The modeas platform is your internal product management proof point:** You conceived, built, and ran a developer tooling platform (200+ builds/hr). That is exactly what this role requires — even if it was for a client.
- **Reframe consulting as embedded engineering:** "My consulting titles are billing designations. My actual operating model — embedded in the client's engineering org, owning the platform, building the team, reporting metrics to the CTO — is identical to an internal Global Head position."

### If they offer to downlevel or ask about title

- Require: clarity on reporting line (must report to Global Head of Engineering, not a layer down), global remit (not just Pune delivery), and a written 12-month VP/Global Head promotion track
